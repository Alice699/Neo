## Design Context

### Users
Neo is for creators, builders, and Web3 teams on Aptos who need a polished way to store, present, and eventually gate large media or data files through Shelby.

### Brand Personality
Precise, luminous, and sovereign. The product should feel technically serious without looking like a generic crypto dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
High-end Web3 infrastructure landing page with an operational product feel: sharp typography, restrained surfaces, clear network controls, and a signature storage-field visual. The app supports both light and dark themes, with Aptos as the preferred local font family.

### Design Principles
- Lead with the product experience, not vague blockchain marketing.
- Make network state and storage mechanics visible and understandable.
- Keep the interface refined, readable, and fast on desktop and mobile.
- Use motion for orientation and feedback, respecting reduced motion settings.
